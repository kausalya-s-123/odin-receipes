Pizza

Description

Classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tomato sauce, and lots of cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until crust is golden.
