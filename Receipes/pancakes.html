Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Soft, fluffy pancakes that are perfect for a weekend breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Don't overmix.
  4. Cook on a greased pan until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
