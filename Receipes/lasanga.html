Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a family favorite layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until brown. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then a layer of noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake for 25–30 minutes.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
